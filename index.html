Our Story

Born from
the need to
express.

Fankaar was born in Lahore — in conversations between students and thinkers who felt they had something to say but nowhere worthy of saying it. We make journals for the Pakistan that dreams quietly, thinks deeply, and creates beautifully.

Why We Exist

Every person is a creator in waiting.

We grew up in a culture that celebrates the engineer, the businessman. But what about the writer, the dreamer? Fankaar is for them — a brand that says your inner world deserves beautiful tools.

What Fankaar Means

"Fankaar" — the artist within.

In Urdu, a fankaar is an artist, a craftsperson. We believe writing — even privately, even imperfectly — is one of the purest forms of art.

Our Craft

Nothing here is accidental.

Every paper weight, binding technique, and cover texture is chosen by writers, for writers. A journal should feel like it belongs in your hands, not in a catalog.

Our Mission

Faith meets creativity.

Our journals sit at the intersection of deen, reflection, and self-expression — made for the Muslim creative who wants to grow in every dimension of life.

Our Manifesto

"We believe expression is not a luxury — it is a necessity. Every mind is an artist. Every story deserves a page. Every voice deserves to be written."
