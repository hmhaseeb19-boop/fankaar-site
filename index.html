Our Story

Born from
the need to
express.

Fankaar was born in Lahore — in conversations between students and thinkers who felt they had something to say but nowhere that felt worthy of saying it. We make journals for the Pakistan that dreams quietly, thinks deeply, and creates beautifully.

Why We Exist

Every person is a creator in waiting.

We grew up in a culture that celebrates the engineer, the doctor, the businessman. But what about the writer, the dreamer, the one who fills margins with thoughts? Fankaar is for them. A brand that says: your inner world deserves beautiful tools.

What Fankaar Means

"Fankaar" — the artist within.

In Urdu, a fankaar is an artist, a craftsperson, a performer. Someone who has surrendered themselves to their art. We named ourselves after this idea because we believe writing — even privately, even imperfectly — is one of the purest forms of art.

Our Craft

Nothing here is accidental.

Every paper weight, every binding technique, every cover texture is chosen by writers, for writers. We test each journal in real creative environments — not boardrooms. Because a journal should feel like it belongs in your hands, not in a catalog.

Our Community

A gathering of creative souls.

On Instagram, in universities, in coffee shops across Pakistan — the Fankaar community is growing. Writers, sketchers, bullet journalers, students with too many thoughts and not enough pages. Join us at @fankaar_pk.

Our Manifesto

"We believe expression is not a luxury — it is a necessity. Every mind is an artist. Every story deserves a page. Every voice deserves to be written."
